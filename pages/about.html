About

Pull back the veil and enter the Strange World, where black cats prowl through your nightmares, ghosts sing in the wind, and women with dark gifts walk among you.

On the surface, everything is as it appears to be, but when you look closer you’ll see the strange and unusual have been gathering, people who sometimes see the world as it truly is, drawn together by forces unseen. At the eye of the storm sit three black cats and who have been sent to walk with humans, and help protect them from their own fears.

Strange & the Familiars storytelling songs guide the audience into the supernatural Strange World, a world in every way like ours with a magical twist.

Combining cleverly-crafted, sweet and salty lyrics, with rock and blues guitar and orchestral cello and drums, Strange leads captivated audiences from a run-of-the-mill neighborhood, to the dark and dazzling dreamscape, and back home where humanity finds itself sitting at a crossroads. Strange & the Familiars' inspirations include the fantastical comic book themes of Neil Gaiman and Alan Moore, the dark humor of Tim Burton, and the dramatic panache of live storytelling.
